What is Med Cabinet?

Med Cabinet is a revolutionary, new way for patients with varying ailmets to find the absolute perfect strain for their needs! You can create a user profile, and save all of our expert recommendations. You can also find local dispensaries in your area where these strains are available, and even have your medicine delivered to your front door!

How does it work?

After you Sign Up, you can search our vast database for strains based on your desired effects. Whether you're looking for medicine to improve your mood, relieve pain, or help with insomnia, we can find the strain for you. Med Cabinet can even tailor your recommendations to a strain's specific type, be it Sativa, Indica or a Hybrid, or a strain's flavor palette.
